I am a Ph.D. candidate of Biological Sciences (Marine Environmental Biology) at the University of Southern California.

My research focuses on how marine phytoplankton adapt to interacting environmental stressors, including ocean warming and nutrient limitation. By integrating laboratory culturing, multi-omics approaches (transcriptomics, proteomics, metallomics), and long-term experimental evolution, I investigate the physiological and molecular mechanisms that influence phytoplankton resilience and their implications for ocean biogeochemistry.
